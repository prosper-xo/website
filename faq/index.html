FAQ

1. WHAT MAKES PROSPER XO DIFFERENT FROM
SPOTIFY OR OTHER STREAMING PLATFORMS?

Prosper XO isn't aiming to replace platforms like Spotify; we
know many artists will still use them; but we are creating tools that let artists earn, connect, own, and build value outside of those systems. Our focus is on giving artists control of their data, direct-to-fan experiences, and meaningful brand partnerships, so streaming isn't their only revenue stream or point of connection.

Instead of competing with DSPs, Prosper XO will provide the infrastructure for artists to thrive on their own terms and stay in control.

2. DO WE CONSULT WITH ARTISTS
WHEN DESIGNING PROSPER XO?

Yes, Prosper XO is built "with and by artists, not just for artists."

HOW WE INVOLVE ARTISTS

  • Monthly Artist Town Halls: Open community discussions, education, and resources for gathering feedback, sharing updates, and listening to artists' needs.
  • One-on-One Conversations: Personal interviews and check-ins with artists across genres and career stages.
  • Advisory + Beta Cohorts: Dedicated groups of artists who test features early and help shape priorities before release.

Artists are considered "co-creators" of the platform, not just "end users." The founding team consists of artists who moved into tech and business, holding advanced training (Master's degrees in Music Business, alumni of Berklee College of Music, NYU, and other top programs) and lived experience as working musicians. This blend ensures the platform is grounded in artist realities while pushing for sustainable innovation.

3. WHAT DOES "GAMIFIED FAN
ENGAGEMENT" MEAN?

It means making fan interaction more rewarding, fun, and interactive. It gives fans ways to actively participate in an artist's journey, turning passive listeners into active allies and participants in building an artist's career, bringing back joy and excitement to the experience.

4. HOW DOES PROSPER XO PROTECT
ARTISTS FROM EXPLOITATION?

Prosper XO commits to never selling out artists' rights, having been created to counter current industry systems that exploit artists' data, labor, and creativity. It aims to build a transparent alternative.

  • Transparent Compensation Models: Clear, fair payouts that artists can see and track.
  • No "Exposure Gigs": Every contribution has value, and the platform does not perpetuate systems of unpaid labor.
  • Data Belongs to the Artist: All first-party data stays with the artist and will never be sold to advertisers, labels, or third parties.
  • Sustainability Over Acquisition: Prosper XO is not built to "flip or sell out to the highest bidder"; it's designed for long-term artist empowerment and sustainable careers.

This structure emphasizes artist collaboration and protection against exploitation, ensuring artists' best interests are at the center.

5. WHAT'S THE ULTIMATE GOAL
OF PROSPER XO?

To help artists build sustainable careers on their own terms by:

  • Keeping more of the value they create.
  • Controlling their data.
  • Building stronger direct fan + brand connections.

6. WHEN DID PROSPER XO OFFICIALLY START?

Officially started in May 2025 in Austin, Texas, but the idea began much earlier from countless late-night conversations and experiences among friends, dreaming of a world where creatives could sustain their creativity and be valued.

Fact: The arts and culture sector generates over $1 trillion annually in the U.S. alone, yet 81% of American musicians live below the poverty line.

7. WHAT IS PROSPER XO?

An artist-first technology platform and cultural policy + education initiative built to shift power back to creators. It provides sustainable income streams, brand partnerships with choice, full ownership and control of data, and gamified fan experiences that deepen connection and joy. The system is designed for transparency, sustainability, and cultural equity, based on the belief that "when artists prosper, culture thrives."

Fact: Music is an economy (U.S. music industry supports nearly 2.5 million jobs, festivals drive billions in tourism/small business revenue).

Fact: In Austin, SXSW + ACL alone generated $912M in 2024, yet nearly half of Austin's musicians live at or below the poverty line, highlighting the need for systemic reform.

8. HOW IS PROSPER XO DIFFERENT FROM SPOTIFY?

Prosper XO is not a streaming service or DSP. Its technology empowers artists through authentic brand partnerships, data ownership, gamified fan engagement, education, legal protection, and sustainable income streams. Streaming is one way artists share work, but not the core. It aims to build tools, insights, and community connections for artists to control their art, data, and future, rather than competing with Spotify or Apple Music.

Fact: Spotify distributes around 70% of its revenue to rights holders (often record labels), who then determine artist pay. The average payout is estimated at $0.006 to $0.008 per stream, with only the top 1% seeing significant earnings.

Fact: Spotify's privacy policies allow them to collect and share vast amounts of user and artist data (demographics, listening behavior) with third parties, without artists having control over how this data is used or monetized.

9. WHERE IS PROSPER XO LAUNCHING THE BETA?

The beta is launching in Austin, TX first, with expansion planned to other cities and online communities soon after.
